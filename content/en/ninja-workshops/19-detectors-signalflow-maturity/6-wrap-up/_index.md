## What we covered

| Phase | Focus |
|---|---|
| **1: Foundations** | **Pyramid / personas**, wizard vs full UI, chart-backed detectors, **pre-flight** |
| **2: SignalFlow** | Boolean streams, **`when()`**, **pipeline traps** |
| **3: Maturity** | **Noise**, **ownership**, **muting**, **tips / LLMs / FAQ** |
| **4: Advanced examples** | **Population** cases, **alerts/events** as metrics, **library** imports, **SPL + O11y** |
